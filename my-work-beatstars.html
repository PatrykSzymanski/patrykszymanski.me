BeatStars – My Library

Product Design

2023

Why

A few words about BeatStars

BeatStars is a digital marketplace designed for music producers, enabling the sale and purchase of beats, loops, and samples. The platform offers music creators a wide range of tools for collaboration, promoting their work, and the opportunity to reach a global community of artists seeking unique sounds for their projects.

Problem

Users of the BeatStars marketplace experienced difficulties in downloading purchased beats and with later access to those files. In the existing flow, users completed the checkout process, seeing a receipt at the end. This caused a burden on the customer support side and poor experiences in the purchase path.

What

My Library is a place where users of the BeatStars Marketplace have access to purchased products. This place was also conceived as a starting point for greater user activation. As the product develops, we will centralize post-purchase actions to this place.

We have attached KPIs and metrics to this solution, which will allow us to decide on further development of the solution, to make it a real center for utilizing purchased beats.

Process

1. Problem statement

We started with the problem statement: "Users of the BeatStars marketplace encounter problems with downloading and accessing purchased files, generating additional tasks for customer support and worsening the purchase experience.

Previously, users had to go through the receipt to download their files.

Initially, I focused on auditing the path and understanding the possibilities for improvement with the Product Owner and engineers.

I conducted short workshops to propose a simple solution in development.
